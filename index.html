Why The Mandalorian Season 1 is Superior to Season 2

By: MistahEye YT G EM

8 minute read

The Mandalorian's freshman season contained an outstanding cast of not only new actors to the Star Wars scene, but also strictly adhereing to characters that had not been previously established in the Star Wars universe. Unfortunately, season 2 often relies on these previously established characters, making it not live up to the original season.
